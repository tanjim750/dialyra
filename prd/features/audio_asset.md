# 13. Audio Asset Management

# Purpose

Audio Asset Management is responsible for storing, organizing, generating, processing, and serving all audio resources used inside Dialyra.

This system manages:

* uploaded audio
* generated TTS audio
* dynamic playback files
* business-specific audio assets
* runtime audio caching

for IVR flows and campaigns.

---

# Core Goals

## 1. Centralized Audio Management

Provide a unified system for all audio assets.

---

## 2. Fast Runtime Playback

Ensure low-latency playback during live calls.

---

## 3. Dynamic Audio Generation

Support:

* TTS generation
* personalized audio
* runtime audio assembly

---

## 4. Business Isolation

Separate audio resources between businesses.

---
